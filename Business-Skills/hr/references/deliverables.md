# HR Deliverables

## Executive level
- Workforce plan summary.
- Talent risk assessment.
- Compensation strategy overview.

## Middle management level
- Hiring plan and progress report.
- Performance calibration summary.
- Training and development plan.

## Frontline level
- Interview scorecards and guides.
- Onboarding checklists.
- Policy FAQ and reminders.

## Strategic to execution coverage
- Strategic: workforce planning, compensation strategy.
- Tactical: hiring and performance cycles.
- Execution: onboarding, interview kits.

## Outline templates
- Workforce plan: demand, supply, gaps, actions.
- Hiring plan: roles, timing, interview capacity.
- Onboarding checklist: steps, owners, dates.
