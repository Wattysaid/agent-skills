# HR Analysis Checklists

## Hiring review
- Validate role scorecards and criteria.
- Check funnel conversion and speed.
- Identify bottlenecks and improve process.

## Performance review
- Confirm calibration consistency.
- Identify development gaps.
- Align outcomes to compensation decisions.

## Retention risk
- Identify teams with elevated attrition.
- Review exit feedback themes.
- Recommend mitigation actions.
