# Handoff Checklists

## HR -> Leadership
- Trigger: talent risks or org design changes identified.
- Provide: risk summary, role gaps, proposed actions.
- Receive: leadership decisions, priorities, sponsorship.

## HR -> PMO
- Trigger: change programs require enablement.
- Provide: training plan, comms needs, timing.
- Receive: rollout milestones, owner assignments.

## HR -> Legal
- Trigger: policy or compliance changes.
- Provide: policy drafts, impacted roles, timeline.
- Receive: legal review, approval steps, updates.
