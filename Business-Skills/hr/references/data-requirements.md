# HR Data Requirements

## Core inputs
- Headcount by team and role.
- Hiring pipeline and time-to-hire.
- Retention and attrition metrics.
- Performance review outcomes.

## Optional inputs
- Compensation bands and offers.
- Training participation and completion.
- Employee engagement survey results.

## Quality checks
- Confirm role and level definitions.
- Align data to current org structure.
- Note missing or outdated entries.
