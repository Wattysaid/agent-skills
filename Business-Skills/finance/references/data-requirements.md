# Finance Data Requirements

## Core inputs
- Income statement, balance sheet, cash flow.
- Cash balance and net burn history.
- Revenue by product, segment, and cohort.
- COGS and gross margin by product.
- Operating expenses by department.

## Optional inputs
- Pipeline forecast and conversion assumptions.
- Pricing and discount policy.
- Headcount plan and compensation ranges.
- Collections and payment terms.

## Quality checks
- Confirm time periods and currency.
- Reconcile totals across statements.
- Note missing or estimated data.
