# Handoff Checklists

## Strategy -> Finance
- Trigger: strategic option needs funding model or ROI gate.
- Provide: objective, horizon, success metrics, assumptions.
- Receive: model, ROI/payback, budget impact, runway impact.

## Finance -> Operations
- Trigger: cost, margin, or efficiency targets require execution changes.
- Provide: cost targets, budget caps, timing constraints.
- Receive: capacity plan, process changes, expected savings.

## Finance -> Sales/Marketing
- Trigger: growth spend or pricing changes affect unit economics.
- Provide: CAC/LTV targets, pricing guardrails, budget limits.
- Receive: pipeline impact, campaign ROI, pricing impact notes.
