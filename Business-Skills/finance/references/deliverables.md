# Finance Deliverables

## Executive level
- Board finance summary: revenue, margin, cash, runway, key risks.
- Capital allocation memo: options, ROI, trade-offs, recommendation.
- Funding readiness brief: metrics, traction, use of funds, runway impact.
- Pricing or margin strategy overview: levers, sensitivities, impact.

## Middle management level
- Monthly forecast update: plan vs actual, revised outlook, drivers.
- Budget variance report: category variances, root causes, actions.
- Cost optimization plan: initiatives, savings, timing, owners.
- Revenue bridge: volume, price, mix, churn, expansion.

## Frontline level
- Expense tracker and policy reminders.
- AR/AP aging follow-up list.
- Invoicing and collections checklist.
- KPI snapshots for team leads.

## Strategic to execution coverage
- Strategic: capital allocation, pricing, long-term runway, funding.
- Tactical: quarterly forecasts, hiring plan impact, margin actions.
- Execution: cash collection, expense control, reporting cadence.

## Outline templates
- Board summary: results, runway, risks, decisions needed.
- Forecast update: plan vs actual, drivers, outlook, actions.
- Cost plan: initiatives, savings, timing, owners.
