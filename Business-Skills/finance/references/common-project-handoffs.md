# Common Project Handoffs

## Product launch
- Strategy -> Marketing: positioning, segment focus, success metrics.
- Marketing -> Sales: messaging, ICP, enablement assets.
- Sales -> Customer Success: success criteria, onboarding plan.
- Operations -> Customer Success: SLA commitments, delivery risks.
- Finance -> Leadership: launch budget, runway impact.

## Pricing change
- Strategy -> Finance: pricing objectives, target segments.
- Finance -> Sales: guardrails, discount policy, ROI.
- Sales -> Marketing: messaging implications, objection handling.
- Legal -> Sales: contract language updates.

## Churn recovery
- Customer Success -> Strategy: churn drivers and segments.
- Strategy -> Operations: operational root causes and fixes.
- Marketing -> Customer Success: lifecycle campaigns and education.
- Finance -> Leadership: revenue impact and investment needs.

## Cost reduction program
- Finance -> Operations: savings targets, timing.
- Operations -> PMO: workstreams, owners, milestones.
- Leadership -> HR: org impacts and change plan.
- PMO -> Leadership: risk and progress reporting.

## Market expansion
- Strategy -> Marketing: target markets and positioning.
- Strategy -> Sales: coverage model and quotas.
- Operations -> Finance: capacity investment needs.
- Legal -> Leadership: regulatory risks and requirements.
