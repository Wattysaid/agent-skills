# Finance Analysis Checklists

## Forecast review
- Validate assumptions and drivers.
- Compare to prior period and plan.
- Stress-test key sensitivities.
- Identify cash impact and runway change.

## Unit economics
- Confirm pricing, COGS, churn.
- Compute margin, LTV, payback.
- Compare to benchmarks or targets.

## Risk scan
- Liquidity risks and covenant risks.
- Concentration risk (customers, vendors).
- Variance drivers with corrective actions.
