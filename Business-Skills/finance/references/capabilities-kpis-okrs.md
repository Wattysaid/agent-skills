# Capabilities, Processes, KPIs, OKRs

## Business capabilities
- Financial planning and analysis (FP&A)
- Cash and liquidity management
- Revenue and margin management
- Cost management and budgeting
- Capital allocation and investment
- Risk and compliance reporting

## Core processes
- Budgeting and forecasting
- Month-end close and variance review
- Cash flow monitoring
- Unit economics and pricing analysis
- Capital request evaluation
- Financial reporting cadence

## Common KPIs
- Gross margin, contribution margin
- Net burn, runway (months)
- EBITDA and operating margin
- Revenue growth and churn impact
- CAC, LTV, payback period
- Forecast accuracy

## Typical OKRs
- Improve gross margin from X to Y by Q4
- Extend runway from X to Y months
- Reduce operating expense variance to <X%
- Increase forecast accuracy to >X%
- Achieve LTV:CAC ratio of X:Y
