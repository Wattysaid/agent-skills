# Leadership Data Requirements

## Core inputs
- Org chart and role definitions.
- Goal/OKR status by team.
- Engagement and retention data.
- Decision and escalation logs.

## Optional inputs
- Performance distribution and calibration.
- Cross-team dependency map.
- Change impact assessments.

## Quality checks
- Confirm ownership and decision rights.
- Align data to current org structure.
- Note missing team inputs.
