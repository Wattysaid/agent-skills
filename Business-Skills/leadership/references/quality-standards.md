# Quality Standards

## Output quality
- Confirm objective, audience level, and decision owner.
- State assumptions and data gaps explicitly.
- Provide quantified impact where possible.
- Include risks, mitigations, and dependencies.
- End with clear next steps and owners.

## Evidence and traceability
- Cite data sources or note when assumptions are used.
- Keep calculations reproducible.
- Separate facts from interpretation.

## Delivery checks
- Use the appropriate deliverable template.
- Tailor depth to executive, middle management, or frontline needs.
- Keep recommendations actionable and time-bound.
