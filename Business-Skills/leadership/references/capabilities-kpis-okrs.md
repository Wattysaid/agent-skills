# Capabilities, Processes, KPIs, OKRs

## Business capabilities
- Organizational design and alignment
- Decision making and governance
- Performance management and coaching
- Culture and engagement
- Change leadership

## Core processes
- Goal setting and OKR alignment
- Leadership communications cadence
- Performance and talent reviews
- Decision rights and escalation
- Change management planning

## Common KPIs
- Employee engagement score
- Retention and attrition rates
- Time to decision
- Goal attainment rate
- Leadership bench coverage

## Typical OKRs
- Increase engagement score by X points
- Reduce attrition to <X%
- Improve OKR completion to >X%
- Cut decision cycle time by X%
- Build succession coverage for X roles
