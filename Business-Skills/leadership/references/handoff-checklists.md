# Handoff Checklists

## Leadership -> HR
- Trigger: org changes, staffing gaps, or policy shifts.
- Provide: role changes, capability needs, timing.
- Receive: hiring plan, performance actions, policy updates.

## Leadership -> PMO
- Trigger: cross-team initiatives need governance.
- Provide: objectives, decision owners, cadence expectations.
- Receive: delivery plan, RAID tracking, reporting format.

## Leadership -> Strategy
- Trigger: misalignment on priorities or goals.
- Provide: business objectives, constraints, culture considerations.
- Receive: refined strategic priorities, KPIs.
