# Leadership Deliverables

## Executive level
- Leadership alignment memo.
- Org design recommendation.
- Culture and engagement summary.

## Middle management level
- OKR alignment map.
- Decision rights matrix.
- Change plan and comms.

## Frontline level
- Team priorities brief.
- 1:1 cadence and agenda.
- Feedback and coaching guide.

## Strategic to execution coverage
- Strategic: org design, leadership model.
- Tactical: alignment, decision rights.
- Execution: routines, coaching, comms.

## Outline templates
- Alignment memo: objective, misalignment, actions, owners.
- Decision rights: decisions, owners, escalation.
- Team priorities: goals, initiatives, support needed.
