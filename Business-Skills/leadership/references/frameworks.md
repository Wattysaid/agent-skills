# Leadership Frameworks

## Core frameworks
- OKRs: objectives and key results.
- RACI: responsible, accountable, consulted, informed.
- 7S: strategy, structure, systems, shared values, skills, style, staff.
- Situational leadership: direct, coach, support, delegate.

## Questions to drive analysis
- Where is alignment breaking down?
- What decisions are unclear or slow?
- Which capability gaps matter most?
