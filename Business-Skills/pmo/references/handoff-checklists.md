# Handoff Checklists

## PMO -> Leadership
- Trigger: portfolio decisions or escalations.
- Provide: status summary, risks, decisions needed.
- Receive: decisions, priority changes.

## PMO -> Operations
- Trigger: execution dependencies or capacity issues.
- Provide: timeline, dependencies, risks.
- Receive: capacity confirmation, delivery constraints.

## PMO -> Finance
- Trigger: budget tracking or funding gates.
- Provide: spend forecast, variance drivers.
- Receive: budget approvals, guardrails.
