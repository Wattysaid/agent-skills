# Capabilities, Processes, KPIs, OKRs

## Business capabilities
- Program governance and reporting
- Planning and dependency management
- Risk and issue management
- Resource and capacity coordination

## Core processes
- Project intake and prioritization
- Plan and milestone tracking
- RAID management and escalation
- Status reporting cadence

## Common KPIs
- On-time delivery rate
- Budget variance
- Scope change rate
- Risk closure rate
- Milestone slippage

## Typical OKRs
- Deliver X% of milestones on time
- Reduce budget variance to <X%
- Close high risks within X days
- Improve status report compliance to >X%
