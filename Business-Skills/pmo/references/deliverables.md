# PMO Deliverables

## Executive level
- Portfolio status summary.
- Risk and dependency overview.
- Investment and resource view.

## Middle management level
- Program plan with milestones.
- RAID summary and mitigation plan.
- Resource allocation report.

## Frontline level
- Status report template.
- Issue log and action list.
- Meeting notes and decisions.

## Strategic to execution coverage
- Strategic: portfolio governance, resource trade-offs.
- Tactical: program plans, risk mitigation.
- Execution: status, issue resolution.

## Outline templates
- Portfolio summary: health, risks, decisions.
- Program plan: milestones, owners, dependencies.
- Status report: progress, risks, next steps.
