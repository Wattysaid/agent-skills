# Handoff Checklists

## Marketing -> Sales
- Trigger: lead flow, messaging, or segment focus shifts.
- Provide: ICP/segment definitions, messaging, MQL/SQL criteria.
- Receive: lead feedback, win/loss insights, objection themes.

## Marketing -> Customer Success
- Trigger: lifecycle messaging and adoption programs need alignment.
- Provide: onboarding content, lifecycle campaigns, education assets.
- Receive: adoption gaps, customer feedback, content requests.

## Marketing -> Finance
- Trigger: budget changes or CAC targets need sign-off.
- Provide: channel ROI, CAC assumptions, spend plan.
- Receive: budget approval, guardrails, reporting cadence.
