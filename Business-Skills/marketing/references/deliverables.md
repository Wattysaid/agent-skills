# Marketing Deliverables

## Executive level
- Marketing strategy memo: segments, positioning, investment, risks.
- Brand health summary: awareness, consideration, sentiment.
- Budget allocation overview: channel ROI and reallocations.

## Middle management level
- GTM plan: launch goals, channels, milestones, owners.
- Campaign performance review: results, insights, next tests.
- Content roadmap: themes, cadence, owners.

## Frontline level
- Campaign briefs and creative checklists.
- Channel playbooks and QA lists.
- Weekly performance snapshot.

## Strategic to execution coverage
- Strategic: positioning, segment focus, budget model.
- Tactical: campaign plans, channel mix, testing.
- Execution: briefs, assets, reporting.

## Outline templates
- Executive memo: objective, key insights, investment, risks, ask.
- Campaign brief: objective, audience, message, offer, channels, KPIs.
- Weekly snapshot: top KPIs, wins, issues, next actions.
