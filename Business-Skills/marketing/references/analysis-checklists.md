# Marketing Analysis Checklists

## Campaign review
- Validate goals and target segment.
- Compare results to benchmarks.
- Identify highest-impact creative and channel.
- Recommend next test and budget shift.

## Positioning review
- Confirm target and competitor set.
- Test message clarity and differentiation.
- Map proof points and gaps.

## Budget review
- Compute ROI and CAC by channel.
- Identify diminishing returns.
- Propose reallocation plan.
