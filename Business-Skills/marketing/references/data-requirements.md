# Marketing Data Requirements

## Core inputs
- Spend by channel and period.
- Funnel metrics by stage.
- Pipeline and revenue attribution.
- Audience and segment performance.

## Optional inputs
- Brand survey and sentiment data.
- Content engagement metrics.
- Competitive share of voice.

## Quality checks
- Confirm attribution model and time window.
- Normalize spend and outcomes by period.
- Note missing segments or channels.
