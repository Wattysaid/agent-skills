# Capabilities, Processes, KPIs, OKRs

## Business capabilities
- Market segmentation and positioning
- Demand generation and acquisition
- Brand and messaging management
- Campaign execution and optimization
- Lifecycle and retention marketing

## Core processes
- GTM planning and channel selection
- Campaign briefing and launch
- Content and creative production
- Performance reporting and optimization
- Attribution and budget allocation

## Common KPIs
- CAC, CPL, ROAS
- CTR, conversion rate
- Pipeline influenced by marketing
- MQL to SQL conversion
- Retention and reactivation rates

## Typical OKRs
- Increase qualified pipeline by X%
- Reduce CAC from X to Y
- Improve conversion rate from X to Y
- Launch X campaigns with >Y% CTR
- Grow brand awareness by X points
