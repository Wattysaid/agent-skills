# Marketing Frameworks

## Core frameworks
- STP: segment, target, position.
- AARRR: acquisition, activation, retention, revenue, referral.
- Messaging ladder: value prop, benefits, proof, features.
- Marketing mix: 4Ps/7Ps.

## Questions to drive analysis
- Which segment has the highest willingness to pay?
- What channel has the best unit economics?
- What message improves conversion most?
