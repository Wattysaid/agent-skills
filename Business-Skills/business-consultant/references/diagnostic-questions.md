# Diagnostic Questions

## Problem clarity
- What decision must be made, by when?
- What does success look like?
- What is in scope vs out of scope?

## Data and evidence
- What data exists and how reliable is it?
- What are the baseline metrics?
- What trends matter most?

## Constraints
- Budget, headcount, or tooling limits?
- Regulatory or contractual constraints?
- Non-negotiable requirements?
