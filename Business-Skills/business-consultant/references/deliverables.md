# Business Consultant Deliverables

## Executive level
- Executive summary: decision, findings, recommendation, risks.
- Board update: outcomes, KPI trends, major decisions needed.
- Investment memo: options, ROI, recommendation.

## Middle management level
- Diagnostic memo: scope, findings, root causes, options.
- Implementation plan: workstreams, milestones, owners.
- KPI framework: definitions, owners, cadence.

## Frontline level
- Workstream plan: tasks, owners, timing.
- Playbooks and checklists.
- Reporting templates.

## Strategic to execution coverage
- Strategic: direction, portfolio choices, value creation.
- Tactical: initiative selection, operating model.
- Execution: delivery plans, scorecards, routines.

## Outline templates
- Executive summary: context, findings, recommendation, risks, ask.
- Diagnostic memo: background, data, findings, options, next steps.
- Implementation plan: workstreams, milestones, dependencies.
