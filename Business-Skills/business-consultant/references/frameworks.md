# Frameworks

Use only when they fit the problem; do not force a framework.

## Strategy and market
- SWOT: Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats.
- Porter's Five Forces: supplier power, buyer power, rivalry, threat of substitution, threat of entry.
- 3C: Company, Customer, Competitor.
- Ansoff Matrix: market penetration, market development, product development, diversification.
- Jobs-to-be-Done: functional, emotional, social jobs; current vs desired outcomes.

## Growth and economics
- Unit economics: CAC, LTV, payback period, gross margin, contribution margin.
- Funnel math: volume * conversion * price, stage-by-stage conversion.
- Growth levers: acquisition, activation, retention, revenue, referral.

## Operations and process
- SIPOC: Suppliers, Inputs, Process, Outputs, Customers.
- Theory of Constraints: identify bottleneck, exploit, subordinate, elevate.
- Lean waste: overproduction, waiting, transport, extra processing, inventory, motion, defects, unused talent.

## Org and change
- RACI: Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed.
- 7S: Strategy, Structure, Systems, Shared values, Skills, Style, Staff.
- Change readiness: urgency, coalition, vision, communication, quick wins.
