# Business Consultant Analysis Checklists

## Hypothesis review
- State hypotheses and evidence.
- Identify data gaps.
- Test sensitivity to assumptions.

## Recommendation review
- Quantify impact and costs.
- Identify risks and mitigations.
- Confirm feasibility and ownership.

## Delivery review
- Tailor to audience level.
- Separate facts vs interpretation.
- Define next steps and cadence.
