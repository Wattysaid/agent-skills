# Handoff Checklists

## Consulting -> Strategy/Finance/Operations
- Trigger: analysis requires domain depth.
- Provide: objectives, hypotheses, data collected.
- Receive: domain analysis, risks, recommended actions.

## Consulting -> Sales/Marketing
- Trigger: growth strategy and GTM decisions.
- Provide: segment focus, growth targets.
- Receive: pipeline impact, positioning, campaign inputs.

## Consulting -> Leadership/PMO
- Trigger: implementation requires governance.
- Provide: recommendation, timeline, owners.
- Receive: execution plan, reporting cadence.
