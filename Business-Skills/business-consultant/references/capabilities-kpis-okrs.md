# Capabilities, Processes, KPIs, OKRs

## Business capabilities
- Problem structuring and hypothesis design
- Diagnostic analysis and insight synthesis
- Stakeholder alignment and decision support
- Change management planning
- Benefits tracking and realization

## Core processes
- Discovery and scoping
- Data gathering and validation
- Option evaluation and recommendation
- Implementation planning and governance
- KPI definition and tracking

## Common KPIs
- Decision cycle time
- Initiative ROI
- Stakeholder alignment score
- Delivery on time and on scope
- Benefits realized vs plan

## Typical OKRs
- Deliver X strategic recommendations with exec approval
- Improve decision cycle time by X%
- Achieve >X% benefits realization
- Increase stakeholder alignment to >X%
