# Executive Decision Brief

## What it is
A one-page format that states the decision, options, recommendation, risks, and ask.

## When to use
- Leadership decisions requiring speed and clarity.

## Why it matters
- Reduces cognitive load for executives.
- Forces crisp trade-offs and a clear ask.

## How to apply
- Use sections: Decision, Context, Options, Recommendation, Risks, Ask.
- Limit to essential metrics and evidence.
