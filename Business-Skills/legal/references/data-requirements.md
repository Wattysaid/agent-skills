# Legal Data Requirements

## Core inputs
- Contract type and key terms.
- Jurisdiction and governing law.
- Policy requirements and obligations.
- Known risks and escalations.

## Optional inputs
- Prior redlines or precedent language.
- Audit results and compliance logs.
- Vendor risk assessments.

## Quality checks
- Confirm document version and parties.
- Note missing clauses and conflicts.
- Record required approvals.
