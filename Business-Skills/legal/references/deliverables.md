# Legal Deliverables

## Executive level
- Legal risk summary for leadership.
- Contract policy and governance update.
- Regulatory readiness overview.

## Middle management level
- Contract review summary with redlines.
- Compliance plan and audit schedule.
- Policy update memo.

## Frontline level
- Clause checklists.
- Approval workflow steps.
- Issue escalation guide.

## Strategic to execution coverage
- Strategic: risk posture, governance model.
- Tactical: contract standards, compliance plans.
- Execution: checklists, approvals, escalations.

## Outline templates
- Risk summary: issue, impact, likelihood, mitigation.
- Contract summary: key terms, issues, next actions.
- Compliance plan: scope, controls, cadence.
