# Handoff Checklists

## Legal -> Sales
- Trigger: contract negotiation or redlines.
- Provide: approved language, non-negotiables, escalation rules.
- Receive: deal context, risks, timing needs.

## Legal -> HR
- Trigger: employment policy updates.
- Provide: legal requirements, draft language, deadlines.
- Receive: rollout plan, training needs.

## Legal -> PMO
- Trigger: compliance checkpoints in delivery.
- Provide: required approvals, audit timing.
- Receive: governance plan, reporting cadence.
