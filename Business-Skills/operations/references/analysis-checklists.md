# Operations Analysis Checklists

## Bottleneck review
- Identify step with highest WIP or wait.
- Confirm constraint impact on throughput.
- Test short-term fixes vs structural fixes.

## Quality review
- Track defects by step and cause.
- Quantify rework cost and time.
- Propose prevention and detection controls.

## Capacity review
- Compare demand vs available capacity.
- Identify variability drivers.
- Recommend staffing or scheduling changes.
