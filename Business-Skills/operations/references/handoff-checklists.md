# Handoff Checklists

## Operations -> Customer Success
- Trigger: SLA changes or delivery constraints impact customers.
- Provide: SLA targets, capacity limits, escalation paths.
- Receive: customer impact priorities, top accounts at risk.

## Operations -> Finance
- Trigger: cost or capacity changes affect budget.
- Provide: cost per unit, headcount needs, timing.
- Receive: budget confirmation, savings targets, payback view.

## Operations -> PMO
- Trigger: multi-team execution needs governance.
- Provide: workstreams, dependencies, owners, timeline.
- Receive: milestone plan, reporting cadence, risk tracking.
