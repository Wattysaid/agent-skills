# Operations Data Requirements

## Core inputs
- Volume by period and channel.
- Cycle time and backlog levels.
- Defect rates and rework counts.
- Staffing levels and schedules.

## Optional inputs
- Vendor or supplier performance.
- Facility and equipment constraints.
- Cost per unit and overtime rates.

## Quality checks
- Confirm time windows and definitions.
- Validate data completeness by step.
- Note measurement gaps and proxies.
