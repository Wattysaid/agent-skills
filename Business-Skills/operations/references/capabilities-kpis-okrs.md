# Capabilities, Processes, KPIs, OKRs

## Business capabilities
- Process design and continuous improvement
- Capacity and workforce planning
- Quality management
- Service level management
- Supply and inventory coordination

## Core processes
- Process mapping and SOP creation
- Demand and capacity planning
- Quality inspection and defect remediation
- SLA monitoring and escalation
- Root cause analysis and improvement tracking

## Common KPIs
- Cycle time and throughput
- First-pass yield and defect rate
- SLA adherence
- Cost per unit
- Backlog size and age

## Typical OKRs
- Reduce cycle time from X to Y
- Improve first-pass yield to >X%
- Achieve SLA adherence of >X%
- Reduce cost per unit by X%
- Cut backlog by X% in Y weeks
