# Operations Deliverables

## Executive level
- Ops performance summary: throughput, quality, cost, risk.
- Capacity and scaling plan: investments, timing, ROI.
- Operational risk review: critical bottlenecks and mitigations.

## Middle management level
- Process improvement plan: bottlenecks, fixes, owners, timeline.
- SLA and quality report: adherence, trends, root causes.
- Staffing and scheduling plan.

## Frontline level
- SOPs and checklists.
- Daily huddle dashboard: volume, backlog, blockers.
- Training and error-prevention guides.

## Strategic to execution coverage
- Strategic: network design, capacity investments.
- Tactical: process redesign, staffing plans.
- Execution: SOPs, shift plans, quality checks.
