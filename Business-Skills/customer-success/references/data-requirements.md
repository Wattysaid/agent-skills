# Customer Success Data Requirements

## Core inputs
- Product usage by account.
- Renewal dates and contract values.
- Support ticket volume and severity.
- Health score components.

## Optional inputs
- NPS or CSAT scores.
- Advocacy and referral activity.
- Executive sponsor engagement.

## Quality checks
- Confirm account IDs and cohorts.
- Align metrics to lifecycle stages.
- Note missing usage tracking.
