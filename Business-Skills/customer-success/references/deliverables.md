# Customer Success Deliverables

## Executive level
- Retention and expansion summary.
- Churn analysis and recovery plan.
- Customer health portfolio view.

## Middle management level
- Onboarding performance report.
- Adoption program plan.
- QBR insights summary.

## Frontline level
- Account playbooks.
- Renewal checklist.
- Escalation and risk log.

## Strategic to execution coverage
- Strategic: retention strategy, expansion focus.
- Tactical: adoption programs, QBR cadence.
- Execution: playbooks, checklists, escalations.

## Outline templates
- Churn analysis: drivers, cohorts, actions.
- QBR: outcomes, ROI, risks, next plan.
- Renewal checklist: stakeholders, status, next steps.
