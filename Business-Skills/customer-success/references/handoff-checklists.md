# Handoff Checklists

## Customer Success -> Sales
- Trigger: renewals or expansion opportunities.
- Provide: health status, stakeholders, expansion timing.
- Receive: account strategy, pricing guidance.

## Customer Success -> Marketing
- Trigger: adoption gaps or lifecycle messaging needs.
- Provide: feature adoption insights, customer stories.
- Receive: enablement content, lifecycle campaigns.

## Customer Success -> Operations
- Trigger: delivery or support issues.
- Provide: SLA breaches, root cause signals, priority accounts.
- Receive: remediation plan, timelines, escalation path.
