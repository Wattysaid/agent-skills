# Customer Success Analysis Checklists

## Health review
- Validate usage, sentiment, support signals.
- Identify top risk accounts and reasons.
- Define intervention playbooks.

## Renewal review
- Confirm renewal dates and stakeholders.
- Review adoption and ROI evidence.
- Identify expansion or discount risks.

## Onboarding review
- Measure time to value.
- Identify adoption bottlenecks.
- Update onboarding steps.
