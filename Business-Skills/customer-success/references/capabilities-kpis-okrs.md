# Capabilities, Processes, KPIs, OKRs

- Onboarding and time-to-value
- Customer health and risk management
- Adoption and expansion programs
- Renewal management

- Onboarding planning and execution
- Health scoring and QBRs
- Adoption playbooks

- Net revenue retention (NRR)
- Logo retention / churn rate
- Time to value
- Product adoption rate
- Renewal rate

- Cut time to value by X%

- Achieve renewal rate of >X%
