## Required intake questions (organization-wide)
- What is the company size, industry, and primary geography?
- What is the business model (pricing, packaging, delivery)?
- What are the top 3 strategic objectives for 12-36 months?
- What are the top 3 risks to growth or profitability?
- What are the current revenue, margin, and growth rates?
- What is the current cash position and runway?
- Which KPIs are board-level and how are they tracked?
- What systems are used for CRM, ERP, marketing, and support?
- Who are the key decision owners by function?
- What initiatives are in flight that affect multiple teams?
