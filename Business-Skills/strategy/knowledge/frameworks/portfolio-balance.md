# Portfolio Balance Matrix

## What it is
A view of initiatives by risk, payoff, and time-to-impact to balance the strategy portfolio.

## When to use
- Multi-initiative planning across horizons.

## Why it matters
- Avoids over-indexing on either short-term wins or long-term bets.

## How to apply
- Classify initiatives into Horizon 1/2/3.
- Review resource allocation and interdependencies.
