# Capabilities, Processes, KPIs, OKRs

## Business capabilities
- Strategic planning and portfolio management
- Market and competitive analysis
- Growth strategy and prioritization
- Business model innovation
- Strategic risk management

## Core processes
- Annual and quarterly strategy cycles
- Market sizing and segmentation
- Option evaluation and trade-off analysis
- Strategic initiative planning
- KPI and milestone governance

## Common KPIs
- Market share and growth rate
- Strategic initiative ROI
- New revenue contribution
- Competitive win rate
- Portfolio health (core/adjacent/new)

## Typical OKRs
- Enter X new segments by Q4
- Grow strategic revenue by X%
- Reduce strategic risk exposure by X%
- Launch X strategic initiatives on time
- Improve competitive win rate to X%
