# Strategy Deliverables

## Executive level
- Strategy memo: objective, options, recommendation, risks.
- Competitive landscape: key threats, differentiation, implications.
- Market entry decision: sizing, timing, investment, gating criteria.
- Portfolio review: keep, grow, divest decisions with rationale.

## Middle management level
- Strategic initiative brief: scope, milestones, owners, dependencies.
- OKR alignment map: objectives to team outcomes.
- Capability gap analysis: current vs required, build vs buy.
- Scenario plan: base, downside, upside with triggers.

## Frontline level
- Team priorities list with rationale.
- Initiative playbook: tasks, timelines, decision points.
- KPI scorecard for execution.

## Strategic to execution coverage
- Strategic: positioning, market selection, portfolio choices.
- Tactical: initiative prioritization, capability plans.
- Execution: KPI tracking, workstream plans, cadence.
