# Handoff Checklists

## Strategy -> Finance
- Trigger: strategic option requires financial viability.
- Provide: options, assumptions, value drivers.
- Receive: ROI/payback analysis, funding options.

## Strategy -> Operations
- Trigger: strategy requires new capabilities or scale.
- Provide: target state, demand assumptions, constraints.
- Receive: capacity plan, process changes, feasibility risks.

## Strategy -> Leadership
- Trigger: strategic change needs org alignment.
- Provide: rationale, priorities, decision rights.
- Receive: comms plan, change risks, leadership actions.
