# Strategy Analysis Checklists

## Option evaluation
- Define options and constraints.
- Compare impact, cost, time, and risk.
- Identify leading and lagging indicators.
- Choose decision criteria and trade-offs.

## Market analysis
- Size TAM/SAM/SOM.
- Validate key segments and needs.
- Assess competitive intensity and barriers.

## Alignment check
- Confirm ownership and decision rights.
- Map dependencies and capability gaps.
- Ensure KPI coverage for outcomes.
