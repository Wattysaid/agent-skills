# Strategy Data Requirements

## Core inputs
- Market size, growth, and segmentation.
- Customer needs, willingness to pay, and retention.
- Competitive landscape and relative positioning.
- Internal capabilities, costs, and constraints.

## Optional inputs
- Regulatory or geographic constraints.
- Partner ecosystem and channel economics.
- Historical initiative performance.

## Quality checks
- State data sources and confidence.
- Note assumptions and unknowns.
- Validate consistency across sources.
