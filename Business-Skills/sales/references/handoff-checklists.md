# Handoff Checklists

## Sales -> Marketing
- Trigger: pipeline shortfall or conversion issues.
- Provide: segment performance, objections, win/loss insights.
- Receive: targeted campaigns, messaging updates, enablement.

## Sales -> Customer Success
- Trigger: new deal closed or expansion planned.
- Provide: success criteria, stakeholders, promises made.
- Receive: onboarding plan, renewal signals, expansion roadmap.

## Sales -> Finance
- Trigger: pricing/discounting or forecast updates.
- Provide: forecast assumptions, deal terms, timing.
- Receive: revenue recognition guidance, guardrails.
