# Sales Data Requirements

## Core inputs
- Pipeline by stage and owner.
- Win/loss records and reasons.
- Average deal size and cycle time.
- Quota and attainment data.

## Optional inputs
- Pricing and discount data.
- Territory or segment performance.
- Enablement usage metrics.

## Quality checks
- Validate stage definitions.
- Confirm time windows and cohorts.
- Reconcile pipeline totals with CRM.
