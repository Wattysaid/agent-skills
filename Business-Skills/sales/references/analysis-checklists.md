# Sales Analysis Checklists

## Forecast review
- Check coverage and stage conversion.
- Identify stalled deals and root causes.
- Compare forecast to historical accuracy.

## Win/loss review
- Segment patterns in wins and losses.
- Identify pricing or product gaps.
- Propose enablement changes.

## Pipeline health
- Validate hygiene and next steps.
- Check stage aging and bottlenecks.
- Balance pipeline across reps.
