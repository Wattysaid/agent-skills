# Capabilities, Processes, KPIs, OKRs

## Business capabilities
- Pipeline management
- Sales execution and forecasting
- Deal qualification and discovery
- Pricing and proposal management
- Sales enablement

## Core processes
- Lead qualification and scoring
- Discovery, proposal, negotiation
- Forecast updates and pipeline reviews
- Win/loss analysis
- Enablement content updates

## Common KPIs
- Win rate and sales cycle length
- Pipeline coverage ratio
- Average deal size
- Forecast accuracy
- Stage conversion rates

## Typical OKRs
- Increase win rate from X to Y
- Reduce sales cycle from X to Y days
- Achieve pipeline coverage of X times quota
- Improve forecast accuracy to >X%
- Increase average deal size by X%
